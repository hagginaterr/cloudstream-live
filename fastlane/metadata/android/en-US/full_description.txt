Twizzle is a Twitch-focused streaming app for Android phones and Android TV.

Features include:
- Live Twitch streams
- Full live-DVR rewind when the current broadcast archive is available
- Live chat at the recommended live position
- Historical replay chat synchronized to the selected VOD timestamp
- Native Twitch, BTTV, FFZ, and 7TV emotes
- Twitch clips and past broadcasts
- Favorites and low-latency playback
- Public-client Twitch device sign-in

Twizzle is an independent open-source fork derived from CloudStream and is not affiliated with or endorsed by Twitch.
